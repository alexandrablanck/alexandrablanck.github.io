About Me

Hi, I'm Alexandra

I'm a writer based in the US with a passion for storytelling in all its forms. By day I work as a Content Manager at Esko, crafting B2B content that makes complex ideas accessible. By night I'm deep in the pages of whatever novel I'm currently writing — or reading. Welcome to my little corner of the internet.

My Work

From thought leadership and industry reports to poetry and fiction, I write across a range of genres and formats. Browse my professional portfolio, published articles, and creative writing on the Me in Print page — and take a peek at the novels I'm currently working on under Books.

Join My Journey

Writing can be a solitary pursuit, but it doesn't have to be. I share the highs and lows of the writing life — querying, drafting, reading, and everything in between — on my Substack and across social media. I'd love to have you along for the ride.
