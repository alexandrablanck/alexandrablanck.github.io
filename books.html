I write contemporary romance and thriller fiction. Here's a look at what I'm currently working on.

Beyond Office Walls
Contemporary Romance
Beyond Office Walls

When travel writer Lucy is paired with photographer Aidan for a high-stakes office competition, there's just one problem — they already know each other. As deadlines blur into late nights and professional rivalry gives way to something undeniable, they're forced to decide if the story they're writing together is worth the risk.

Our Mother's Sins
Thriller & Mystery
Our Mother's Sins

When a volatile mother is arrested for the murder of her husband, her two children do the only thing they know how — run. Told across two timelines and through the eyes of an eldest daughter carrying secrets she was never meant to keep, and the zany neighbors who watched it all unfold, this thriller unravels the truth about what really happened inside those walls.
